Basic Info About Me

I am currently a sophomore at UC Berkeley majoring in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science (EECS) and Business Administration, and I am minoring in Mathematics. My specific interests include theoretical computer science, computer vision, machine learning, quantitative finance, business analytics, and data-driven market research. I've interned at companies including NVIDIA and Capital One, and will intern at Amazon as a software development engineering (SDE) intern this summer. I've also served as a consultant for companies including Red Cross, Illy Coffee, Lululemon, and Nielsen through Voyager Consulting, where I am a strategy consultant and technical manager. After graduating, I plan on furthering my education by pursuing a Master's degree in Computer Science. I am currently interested in research-based software and data engineering as well as quantitative analyst roles to gain more experience and work with professionals in the field.
